# Skill: Hooks Library (Swipe File)

Proven hook patterns for Summit Build Co. Use these as scaffolding, then make
every hook brand-specific with a real proof point or local reference. Never
ship a hook that would work for any builder.

## FEAR OF COST BLOWOUTS
- "The quote was $480k. The final bill was $560k. Here's how to never let that happen."
- "Most build contracts hide the blowout in two words: 'provisional sum'."
- "The price you sign should be the price you pay. Full stop."

## FEAR OF THE BUILDER DISAPPEARING
- "Your builder going under shouldn't be your risk to carry."
- "19 years in the Hunter Valley. We're not going anywhere — and your deposit isn't either."
- "Half-built frames in the rain are someone's reality right now. Let's make sure it's never yours."

## SOCIAL PROOF / TRACK RECORD
- "200 homes. 19 years. One region. That's not luck — that's a system."
- "Ask around Maitland. Our reputation got here before this ad did."

## ASPIRATION (use sparingly, always grounded)
- "The home you've been sketching on the back of envelopes — let's price it properly."
- "Standing on your own slab at sunset hits different when you know the final number won't move."

## TRUST / PROCESS
- "You'll have the director's mobile number, not a ticket number."
- "Walk your site any day you like. It's your home — you should be able to see it."

## HOOK CONSTRUCTION CHECKLIST
1. Does it name a specific fear or desire in the first 6 words?
2. Is there a concrete number or local detail?
3. Could a competitor run it unchanged? If yes — rewrite.
4. Under ~12 words and readable at a glance?
