## WHO WE ARE

Summit Build Co is a family-owned residential building company in the Hunter
Valley, NSW. We have been building homes for **19 years** and have completed
**200+ custom homes, knockdown-rebuilds and major renovations** across
Maitland, Cessnock, Pokolbin, Newcastle and the surrounding region.

We are not a volume builder. We build a limited number of homes each year so
every client gets the director's personal phone number and a site they can
walk through any time.

## WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT (PROOF POINTS)

- **Fixed-Price Guarantee** — the price you sign is the price you pay. No
  "provisional sums" that balloon halfway through the slab.
- **19 years, 200+ homes** in one region. We are not going anywhere.
- **Director-led** — you deal with the people who own the company, not a
  rotating cast of subcontracted project managers.
- **Local trades, local reputation** — our subbies have worked with us for
  years; our reputation is the only marketing that has ever mattered here.
- **Free, no-obligation site assessment** — we walk your block before you
  commit a dollar.

## WHO WE TALK TO (AUDIENCE)

Hunter Valley families and professionals, typically 30–55, who are:

- Building their first custom home or knocking down and rebuilding.
- Burned by — or terrified of — **cost blowouts** they have heard about from
  friends.
- Afraid of the horror story: the **builder who disappears**, goes under, or
  leaves a half-finished frame in the rain.
- Time-poor and craving a builder who communicates without being chased.

### Core pain points we speak to

1. "What if the final bill is nothing like the quote?"
2. "What if the builder goes broke and takes my deposit?"
3. "What if I can never get a straight answer about where my money went?"
4. "I don't want to project-manage tradies on my weekends."

## VOICE & TONE

- **Plain-spoken and confident.** We talk like a straight-shooting builder
  on site, not a marketing department.
- **Calm authority.** We never hype, never use exclamation-mark spam, never
  promise the world. We reassure with specifics.
- **Proof over adjectives.** "200 homes in 19 years" beats "trusted and
  reliable." Always reach for the concrete number.
- **Australian, regional, grounded.** Hunter Valley references are welcome.
  No American spelling. No corporate jargon ("synergy", "solutions",
  "bespoke journey").
- **Honest about fear.** We name the worry the reader already has, then show
  exactly why it cannot happen with us.

### Banned language

Luxury-for-its-own-sake fluff, "dream home" clichés used hollowly, fake
urgency ("ONLY 2 SPOTS LEFT!!"), anything we cannot literally back up.

## THE OFFER

Primary call to action: **Book a free, no-obligation site assessment.**
Secondary: **Download the Fixed-Price Guarantee explainer** / **See the last
10 homes we built.**

## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE (for image prompts)

- Real, warm, natural-light photography — not glossy stock or CGI sheen.
- Hunter Valley setting: rural-edge blocks, gum trees, big skies, brick and
  cladding, rendered facades, Colorbond roofs.
- Honest construction moments are gold: a builder and a young family standing
  on a slab, framing against a sunset, handing over keys at the front door.
- Palette: warm charcoal, natural timber, soft amber/gold light. Avoid cold
  blue corporate tones.
- Aspect: square (1:1) for the feed. Leave calm negative space for text.
- Mood: trustworthy, established, calm, premium-but-grounded.

## NON-NEGOTIABLES

Every line of copy must be impossible to lift and paste onto a generic
builder's ad. If you could swap in any other company name and it still works,
it is not on-brand. Reach for Summit-specific proof every time.
